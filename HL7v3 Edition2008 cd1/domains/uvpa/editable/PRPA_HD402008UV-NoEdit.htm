PRPA_HD402008UV02
Linked Inpatient Encounters		Description:

This R-MIM defines the message used to report that inpatient encounters for two different patients are linked (or unlinked) for care and billing purposes.

Design Comments:

An interaction between a patient and care provider(s) for the purpose of providing healthcare-related service(s)

 
Design Comments:

The 'source' encounter is in some way dependent on the 'target' encounter. For example, a new born baby's encounter is linked to the mother's encounter, or an organ donor's encounter is linked to the organ recipient's encounter.
